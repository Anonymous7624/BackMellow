Shipping Info

Last updated: April 2026

We want your BackMellow to arrive as quickly as possible. Here's a clear overview of how our shipping works, what to expect, and how to get help if you need it.

Estimated U.S. Delivery: 8–12 Business Days — from the date of your order. Delivery times may vary slightly by location and carrier.

How Shipping Works

When you place an order, here's what happens from purchase to your door:

1
Order Placed

Your order is confirmed and sent to our fulfillment team. You'll receive an order confirmation at the email address you provide at checkout.

2
Processing (1–3 Business Days)

Your order is picked, packed, and prepared for shipment. We process orders on business days (Monday through Friday, excluding holidays).

3
In Transit

Your package is handed off to the carrier and is on its way. Depending on your location within the U.S., transit typically takes 5–9 additional days.

4
Delivered to Your Door

Your BackMellow arrives. If you receive a tracking number, you can follow your package's progress with the carrier directly.

Estimated Delivery Window

For orders shipped within the United States, the typical delivery window is 8–12 business days from the date of purchase. This estimate accounts for processing time and carrier transit.

Delivery times can vary depending on:

  • Your delivery address and proximity to shipping hubs
  • The carrier assigned to your shipment
  • Seasonal volume increases (such as holidays)
  • Unexpected carrier delays or weather events

We appreciate your patience — we always aim to get your order to you as quickly as possible.

Tracking Your Order

Where available, tracking information will be provided once your order has shipped. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking details to the email address on your order.

If you haven't received tracking information or have questions about your shipment's status, please email support@backmellow.com with your order information and we'll look into it right away.

Delays

While we do everything we can to ensure timely delivery, occasional delays can occur due to carrier issues, weather events, high shipping volumes, or other factors outside our control.

If your order has not arrived within the expected window and tracking shows no recent updates, please contact us at support@backmellow.com and we'll do our best to assist you in resolving the issue quickly.

Where We Ship

We currently ship to addresses within the United States. The 8–12 day estimate applies to continental U.S. orders. Delivery to Alaska, Hawaii, or U.S. territories may take additional time.

International shipping availability is not currently confirmed. If you're outside the U.S. and interested, email us at support@backmellow.com and we'll let you know if we can help.

Shipping Support

If you have any questions about your shipment, our team is happy to help.

  • Missing or delayed package
  • Incorrect delivery address
  • Damaged item on arrival
  • Package marked delivered but not received

For any of the above, email support@backmellow.com and include your order information. We respond within 1–2 business days.

Need Help With Your Shipment?

Our support team is here to help with anything shipping-related. Please include your name and order details when you reach out so we can assist you as efficiently as possible.
